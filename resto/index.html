Our Story

A legacy of culinary excellence

Founded in 2025 by Chef Rajan Yadav , Royal Feast began as a passion project to bring world-class dining to Prayagraj. What started as a small 30-seat restaurant has now blossomed into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, respect traditional techniques while embracing innovation, and create memorable experiences for every guest. We work closely with local farmers and global suppliers to bring you seasonal menus that celebrate the best flavors from around the world.

The Royal Feast team includes award-winning chefs, expert mixologists, and hospitality professionals dedicated to making your visit exceptional. From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, we're honored to be part of your special moments.
